About Us

From collecting the waste of flex banners, multiple steps are involved in converting them into carry bags.

Step 1: We carefully separate dust particles from the banners and clean them thoroughly.

Step 2: After cleaning, we ensure the banners are strong enough to hold weight and are durable.

Step 3: Banners are cut into various sizes based on market demand and stitched into carry bags using strong, durable threads.

Step 4: Bags are customized to fit customer requirements, and they come in multiple sizes.

Benefits: These flexi bags are waterproof, durable for months, and capable of holding heavy objects that typical bags cannot.

In addition to their strength, these bags contribute to environmental sustainability. Customers can return used bags and purchase more as part of our eco-friendly initiative.
